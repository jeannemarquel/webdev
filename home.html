OUR STORY

The idea of setting up a cozy place that would serve tasty, hearty meals with both vegetarian and vegan dishes,
and to present the food as great comfort food but with no meat is something new for Manila.
Corner Tree Cafe is a place that serves simple, down-to-earth, good food and also offers great desserts.
We serve food from Asia to Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and the Americas in a cozy intimate setting.
You don’t have to be a vegetarian to enjoy our delicious food. In fact, most of our customers are omnivores,
but vegetarians will feel right at home here. Through our growing consciousness, we are able to make better choices
for our health, the animals and our planet’s well-being.
